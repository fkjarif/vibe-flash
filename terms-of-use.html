Privacy Policy

Effective: January 1, 2026 ~4 min read Also see: Terms of Use →

The short version: we collect nothing.

VibeFlash has no backend, no analytics, no cookies, and no user accounts. Everything stays in your browser. We literally cannot see your data because it never reaches us. This policy explains the technical specifics of how that works.

Section 01

No Data Collection

VibeFlash does not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your data on our servers — because we don't have any servers that receive user data.

  • No cookies. We do not set any first-party or third-party cookies.
  • No analytics. We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Plausible, or any other analytics service.
  • No server-side logs of user activity. Netlify may log standard HTTP access data (IP addresses, timestamps) as part of its hosting infrastructure — this is governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • No tracking pixels or fingerprinting. We do not embed any invisible tracking technology.
  • No advertising. VibeFlash displays no ads and shares no data with ad networks.
Section 02

Local Storage (IndexedDB)

All data created within VibeFlash is stored exclusively in your browser using the IndexedDB Web API — a structured local database built into every modern browser.

The following data is stored locally in your browser and nowhere else:

  • Flashcard decks — names and creation timestamps
  • Flashcards — front text (question), back text (answer), ordering
  • OpenRouter API key — if you choose to use AI generation (see Section 3)
  • App preferences — such as your preferred color theme (dark/light mode), stored via localStorage

Section 03

Third-Party API (OpenRouter)

The AI flashcard generation feature is entirely optional. If you choose to use it, here is exactly what happens:

  1. You supply your own OpenRouter API key, which is saved to your browser's IndexedDB.
  2. When you click "Generate Flashcards," your browser makes a direct HTTPS request from your device to OpenRouter's API endpoint (api.openrouter.ai), including a portion of your extracted PDF text and your API key.
  3. OpenRouter processes the request using the AI model you selected and returns the generated flashcards.
  4. VibeFlash stores the returned flashcards in your browser's IndexedDB.

We never see it. The creator of VibeFlash does not receive, log, intercept, or store any data from your OpenRouter API calls — not your API key, not your PDF text, not the AI response. The request goes directly from your browser to OpenRouter.

By using the AI generation feature, you agree to OpenRouter's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, which govern how they handle data sent to their API. Please review those documents to understand how OpenRouter processes your information.

Section 04

No Personal Information

VibeFlash does not ask for, require, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No name. We do not ask for your name anywhere in the app.
  • No email address. There is no registration, sign-up, or mailing list.
  • No phone number. Not required or collected.
  • No payment information. VibeFlash is free. We do not process payments.
  • No location data. We do not request or access your geographic location.
  • No device fingerprinting. We do not collect browser version, screen resolution, hardware identifiers, or similar data for tracking purposes.

The only data in the system is what you explicitly create: your flashcard content. And it lives only on your device.

Section 05

Children's Privacy

VibeFlash is a general-purpose study tool suitable for users of all ages. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow shared personal data through VibeFlash, please be aware that VibeFlash has no mechanism to receive such data — all content remains local to the browser. There is no information for us to delete from our systems, because none exists on our end.

If you have concerns, feel free to contact us at the address in Section 7, and we will clarify any aspect of how the app works.
